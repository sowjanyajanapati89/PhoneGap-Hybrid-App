About Do-it mobile app

Do-it app makes it easy for anyone to volunteer in their community, by enabling them to post, search, apply and deliver volunteering work. Having a Volunteer CV functionality means that volunteers can record their activities, helping to build a profile, which can be used to supplement career aspirations. The app also makes it simple and affordable for charities and community group to recruit staff and for volunteers to turn their voluntary work into paid work. Do-it mobile app, has been developed as part of the EU project - CROSS (http://www.crossproject.eu/en/) by Sparta Digital, an integrated IT consultancy dedicated to social causes and developments.

 

About Do-it website

Do-it is the UK's national volunteering database. We list volunteering opportunities from thousands of charities and social action groups throughout the UK, from the largest household names through to small informal groups who've come together because they want to change something in their local area.
